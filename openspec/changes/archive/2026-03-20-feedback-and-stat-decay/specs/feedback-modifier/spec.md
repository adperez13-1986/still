## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Feedback modifier has slot-dependent behavior
The Feedback slot modifier card SHALL produce a different effect depending on which body slot it is assigned to. Feedback SHALL be assignable to any slot that has equipment (universal placement, same restriction as Repeat).

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to HEAD
- **WHEN** Feedback is assigned to HEAD and HEAD fires during execution
- **THEN** for each card drawn by HEAD this turn, Arms gains +2 bonus damage on its next fire this execution (tracked via `feedbackArmsBonus` on CombatState)

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to TORSO
- **WHEN** Feedback is assigned to TORSO and TORSO fires during execution
- **THEN** 75% of block gained by TORSO (rounded down) SHALL be dealt as damage to a random non-defeated enemy

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to ARMS
- **WHEN** Feedback is assigned to ARMS and ARMS fires during execution
- **THEN** 33% of damage dealt by ARMS (rounded down) SHALL heal the player (cannot exceed max HP)

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to LEGS
- **WHEN** Feedback is assigned to LEGS and LEGS fires during execution
- **THEN** block generated by LEGS SHALL persist to the next turn instead of resetting

#### Scenario: Feedback bonus resets each execution
- **WHEN** a new execution phase begins
- **THEN** `feedbackArmsBonus` SHALL be reset to 0

### Requirement: Persistent block from Legs Feedback decays
Block carried over from Legs Feedback SHALL decay by 25% at the start of each turn before new block is added.

#### Scenario: Persistent block decay calculation
- **WHEN** a new turn starts and the player has carried block from Legs Feedback
- **THEN** carried block is reduced to `floor(carriedBlock * 0.75)` before any new block is generated

#### Scenario: Persistent block equilibrium
- **WHEN** Legs equipment generates 5 block per turn with Feedback
- **THEN** carried block approaches an equilibrium of approximately 15-17 total block over many turns

### Requirement: Feedback card definition
Feedback SHALL be a slot modifier card available in the S1 card pool.

#### Scenario: Feedback card properties
- **WHEN** the Feedback card is defined
- **THEN** it SHALL have: energy cost 2 (upgraded: 1), type slot modifier, subtype feedback, universal slot placement, no exhaust keyword

#### Scenario: Feedback in card rewards
- **WHEN** a combat encounter is cleared in any sector
- **THEN** Feedback SHALL be eligible to appear in the card reward selection from S1 onward
