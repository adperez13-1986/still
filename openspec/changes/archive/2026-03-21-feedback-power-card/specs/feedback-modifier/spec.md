## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Feedback card definition
Feedback SHALL be a freePlay system card available in the S1 card pool. When played, it permanently applies the Feedback effect to the chosen slot for the rest of combat.

#### Scenario: Feedback card properties
- **WHEN** the Feedback card is defined
- **THEN** it SHALL have: energy cost 3 (upgraded: 2), type system, freePlay true, Exhaust keyword, universal slot targeting (any slot with equipment)

#### Scenario: Feedback in card rewards
- **WHEN** a combat encounter is cleared in any sector
- **THEN** Feedback SHALL be eligible to appear in the card reward selection from S1 onward

### Requirement: Feedback modifier has slot-dependent behavior
Feedback SHALL produce a permanent combat-long secondary effect on the chosen body slot. The effect activates every execution phase for the rest of combat without requiring a card to be assigned to the slot.

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to HEAD
- **WHEN** Feedback is played targeting HEAD
- **THEN** for the rest of combat, each time HEAD fires, Arms gains +2 bonus damage per card drawn by HEAD that execution (tracked via `feedbackArmsBonus` on CombatState)

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to TORSO
- **WHEN** Feedback is played targeting TORSO
- **THEN** for the rest of combat, each time TORSO fires, 75% of block gained by TORSO (rounded down) SHALL be dealt as damage to a random non-defeated enemy

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to ARMS
- **WHEN** Feedback is played targeting ARMS
- **THEN** for the rest of combat, each time ARMS fires, 33% of damage dealt by ARMS (rounded down) SHALL heal the player (cannot exceed max HP)

#### Scenario: Feedback assigned to LEGS
- **WHEN** Feedback is played targeting LEGS
- **THEN** for the rest of combat, block generated by LEGS SHALL persist to the next turn

#### Scenario: Feedback bonus resets each execution
- **WHEN** a new execution phase begins
- **THEN** `feedbackArmsBonus` SHALL be reset to 0

#### Scenario: Feedback does not occupy modifier slots
- **WHEN** Feedback is played on a slot
- **THEN** the slot's primary and secondary modifier positions SHALL remain available for other modifier cards

#### Scenario: Feedback on disabled slot
- **WHEN** Feedback is played targeting a slot that is currently disabled
- **THEN** the play SHALL succeed and the persistent effect SHALL activate when the slot fires on future turns

#### Scenario: Multiple Feedback cards on same slot
- **WHEN** a second Feedback card is played targeting a slot that already has persistent Feedback
- **THEN** the play SHALL succeed (exhausting the card and spending energy) but have no additional effect — Feedback does not stack

### Requirement: Persistent block from Legs Feedback decays
Block carried over from Legs Feedback SHALL decay by 50% at the start of each turn before new block is added.

#### Scenario: Persistent block decay calculation
- **WHEN** a new turn starts and the player has carried block from Legs Feedback
- **THEN** carried block is reduced to `floor(carriedBlock * 0.50)` before any new block is generated

#### Scenario: Persistent block equilibrium
- **WHEN** Legs equipment generates 5 block per turn with permanent Feedback
- **THEN** carried block approaches an equilibrium of approximately 10 total block over many turns
