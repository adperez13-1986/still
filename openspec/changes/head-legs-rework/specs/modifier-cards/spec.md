## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Spark filler card

#### Scenario: Spark card played
- **WHEN** Spark (Override, 2E) is assigned to any slot
- **THEN** the slot deals 4 damage to one enemy. This is deliberately weaker than Emergency Strike (8 AoE) to incentivize card removal and replacement.

### Requirement: Patch Job filler card

#### Scenario: Patch Job card played
- **WHEN** Patch Job (Override, 2E) is assigned to any slot
- **THEN** the slot gains 6 Block. This is deliberately weaker than Emergency Shield (12 Block) to incentivize card removal and replacement.
