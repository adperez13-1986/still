## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: Link synergy activation
**Reason:** Type-based pair synergies are replaced by card + equipment + part combos (the deckbuilder's native form of synergy). Players build identity by assembling complementary cards/equipment/parts, not by arranging types into pairs.

### Requirement: Type-based synergy table
**Reason:** The 18-entry synergy table is deleted. The rich interaction space of the deckbuilder (95 cards × 20 equipment × 13 parts) replaces it.
